PORSCHE INSURANCE

PORSCHE CAR INSURANCE

It was founded in 2009 by Porsche Bank AG based in Salzburg, Austria. Our main task is to take care and secure your vehicle.

Whether it is a new or used vehicle, our goal is to offer all the services in one place to make your ride safe and economical.

LIABILITY INSURANCE

The automobile liability insurance policy insures your liability towards third parties for damage caused by the use of a motor vehicle in your country and abroad.

Liability insurance allows the injured party to claim compensation directly from the insurer, without causing the claimant any material liability.

CASCO INSURANCE

Casco insurance covers the risks of a car accident, theft, hail, fire, explosion, manifestations and demonstrations, robberies.

Casco insurance of your vehicle allows you to receive compensation for material damage caused by a car accident even when you caused the car accident.
