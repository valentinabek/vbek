'Open door days' of Porsche Inter Auto in Pula

Porsche Inter Auto is organizing Open Door Days in Pula from October 9 to 13.

Visitors will find many special benefits, new and used cars, and the introduction of new car models.

From Tuesday to Saturday, a new Volkswagen and a redesigned Skoda salon that meets world standards can be seen.

There are also trial runs of the latest car models from world-renowned brands Volkswagen, Skoda, Porsche, Seat and Audi.

On Open Door Days, special additional benefits will be available for purchasing a vehicle, arranging an assessment of your used vehicle,

discuss financing models, buy a new or used car, and also book an appointment for service.

New Volkswagen car models will be unveiled during the Open Day. Porsche Inter Auto Open Days in Pula, 2B Industrial Road,

are held from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.
