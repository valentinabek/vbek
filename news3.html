PORSCHE INSURANCE

Sale of used vehicles in Porsche Inter Jankomir salon is starting!

In the company showroom at Jankomir, potential buyers are offered over 500 vehicles of all brands and categories at special prices this Friday and Saturday only.

What's new is a special 3/3 (third-third-third) financing offer with literally 0% interest and no additional fees.

This way you can get the 2009 Audi A5 Convertible for as little as 70.000kn, or the prestigious 2013 CLS Audi for 130.000kn.

Particularly interesting is the already traditional bidding. The VW Golf Variant is now available with a starting price of only 1.000 kn.

In parallel with the sale, there will be a promotion of Seat's first SUV model, the attractive Ateca, whose capabilities anyone will be able to try out on the prepared demo.

The Porsche Inter Auto Croatia showroom is located at Jankomir, Velimir Skorpika 21-23, working days from 08:00am to 04:30pm and on Saturdays from 10:00am to 08:00pm.
